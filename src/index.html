Crime rate down in most Seattle neighborhoods

The crime rate has gone down in more than half of Seattle’s neighborhoods since the start of the decade. While Pioneer Square had the biggest decline, due to a sharp drop in the property crime rate, it also had the city’s largest increase in violent crime rate.
